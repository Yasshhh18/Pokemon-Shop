Pikachu

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mouse Pokémon. Pikachu evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone. Pikachu is one of the most recognizable Pokémon and serves as the franchise's mascot.
